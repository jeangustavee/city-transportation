FAQ



What hours do you operate?

We are open 24 hours a day. You can book your reservation for any time of the day or night.

What are your rates?

See our rate estimator on our Order Online page. If your destination is not available on the rate estimator form, get in touch with us directly.

Do you accept credit cards?

Absolutely. City Transportation accepts the following credit cards:
• (AMEX) American Express
• MasterCard
• Visa
• Discover

We also accept Traveler’s Checks. We do not accept personal checks. The passenger can pre-pay using the online reservation form, or pay by card or cash when the vehicle arrives.

Does City Transportation provide flat rates for long distance trips out of the Jacksonville area?

Yes, City Transportation does provide flat rates for long distance trips or trips over 25 miles. Please use our Rate Estimator, or give call or email us if you want to know the rate for your trip.

What is the best way to avoid a no-show?

Our automated online reservation system eliminates the chances of a no-show. When a reservation time is approaching, the dispatch center is notified, and the vehicles are sent out to pick you up on-time. But for your satisfaction, you may reconfirm your ride by phone an hour or two before your pickup.

Should I call to confirm the reservation if I use the online order form?

The online reservation form is an automated system, and so it is not necessary to call to confirm. If you wish to talk to us anyway, we can be reached at 904-111-2222.

Should I call to confirm the reservation if I use the online order form?

Depending on how you use our online reservation form, you will receive instant feedback through the web, and email, or an SMS text message.
